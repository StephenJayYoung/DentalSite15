Meet the Team

Chris Mueller, DDS

General Dentist

Dr. Chris Mueller, a Port Orchard dentist that specializes in treating those with complex dental and cosmetic needs, graduated from the prestigious University of Washington School of Dentistry. He continues his education in cosmetic dentistry at the world renowned Kois Center, and was profiled in a book by Dr. Bill Blatchford about 23 top leaders in the dental field, titled “Playing You’re A Game.” Further hands-on training with leaders in the field of implant dentistry has allowed him to place dental implants and crowns, one of the only dentists in the West Sound with this training. So whether you need advanced treatment or a routine cleaning, you can be assured that all your dentistry will be performed with the utmost precision and care.

A Washington native, Dr. Mueller and his wife Natalie have five children.

It is such a great feeling to be excited about where you work, who you work with and what you are accomplishing in your work day. If you are in search of a dental care team you will consider our office & give us a try. There is a reason we’ve been rated #1 Dentist in Port Orchard over the last decade!

Smile Makeovers

Veneers

Cosmetic Dentures


Do you wear traditional dentures or have a mouthful of missing or failing teeth? Eat what you want again, smile, laugh, and look and feel years younger with our cosmetic dentures and implant-supported dentures.

Composite Bonding


Composite resin is a white, tooth colored material that can be shaded to match your teeth. This material can give you a low-cost improvement in your smile.

Root Canal Therapy

Periodontal Therapy

Click for more information

Periodontal Therapy


Gum disease is treated by carefully removing the bacteria and substances that form in the pockets around the teeth. The removal of this material occurs on a microscopic level and requires great skill. Our dental team has had advanced training to effectively remove all of the bacteria.

Porcelain Onlays

Click for more information

Porcelain Onlays


Dr. Mueller strives to conserve your own healthy tooth structure whenever possible. For those who have worn or cracked teeth, or who have large silver fillings, Dr. Mueller recommends porcelain onlays. The porcelain bonds directly to the tooth enamel which means it is durable and beautiful. All of Dr. Mueller's family and dental team members have porcelain onlays in their mouths since we believe it is the best option available to protect your smile.

Smile-Whitening

Snap-On Smile®


Snap-On Smile® is an affordable, non-invasive, and completely reversible cosmetic removable arch that fits over your existing teeth. It can easily and painlessly give you a beautiful smile. Call us for a free consultation.

Digital Practice

Digital Practice


Our use of digital x-ray technology and 3D i-CAT® technology reduces your radiation exposure and eliminates the use of chemicals. We can see and treat problems long before they become visible on traditional x-rays. The increased precision means saving you time and money and avoiding complications with your treatments.
